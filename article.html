Denver Trip with the Leaders!

Written by Katie. October 1st, 2022
Leadership

The leaders recently had the Leadership Summit in Denver. This was a great opportunity for those of us TSE’s in Denver to get to know our leaders better. We were able to spend a couple of days in office with them learning and team building. The leaders also all got to be in person for the town hall with Steve!

On Wednesday we were able to take our team building to the next level and outside of the office. We went to Oskar Blues for dinner a local brewery here in Denver.We were joined by some old friends of the desk,Rocco and Larry. Léma got to try her first American Buffalo Wings and we also tried Elk Sausage to feel adventurous. Personally, I will stick with Léma on the Buffalo Wings.

After dinner we went to the new MeowWolf Experience, Convergence Station. Convergence Station takes you through 4 sci-fi worlds that blow your mind from the moment you step out of the elevator. You can touch everything, there are no maps, and you really become a part of the art. There are door and mirrors all around and getting lost is inevitable. We were all able to embrace our inner child while we explored this amazing museum. This was an unforgettable, mind - bending experience. A must see when you are in the Denver Area.

Keep an eye our for more in-person opportunities! These chances really transform the team and open you up to amazing team building options!
