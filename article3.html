Tech Tips

DO

Let the client know that each new issue would require a new case be created. You can even use the following verbiage in an email if necessary:

  • ○ “Since this appears to be a new issue/inquiry and not a part of our current case, we do ask that a new ticket be created for this for auditing purposes and to ensure that it is routed properly. Would you like for me to create this case for you?”

If on a client call that is nearing its conclusion, let the client know that a new issue would require a new case, but you can offer to create the case offline and either keep it in your name or route it to the correct queues for assignment. If the new concern relates to a specialized product and is urgent, you can use that team’s chatter handle and reach out to your manager.

DON'T

Dismiss the client’s newest concern. Tell the client that you don’t know how to help. Instead let the client know that you will “transition the case to the correct team for resolution.”
